Drinktober

Back in 2017, my friend Heidi signed up for a sponsored "Go Sober for October" challenge in aid of Macmillan Cancer Support To show my "support", I immediately pledged to post a photo of myself having a different drink every day on her facebook timeline.

This was an immediate hit with our mutual friend groups and so by popupar demand Drinktober returned in 2018, now with themed photos and costumes to keep things interesting.

The 2019 iteration gained its own web presence and pulled out all the stops with more costumes and photoshop work, culminating in a week of horror-themed beers in the lead up to "Drinkowe'en"

See you around!
